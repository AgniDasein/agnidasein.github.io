BIO

  AGNI DASEIN

is a digital artist, painter based in Vienna, Austria.

EXHIBITIONS

August 2019, club "Blank" factory "Arsenal", St.Petersburg, Russia. Group exhibition.
July 2013, Künstlerbund Graz, Austria. Solo exhibition.

September 2005 - June 2010, Ural Federal University.
Yekaterinburg, Russia. Economist's degree.

PROJECTS

2016-2018, worldwide project. “The museums and visitors”.

Actionism “The social suicide”.

FESTIVALS

FILM

NEWS

Eden 2020.
The video shows the connection between classic painting and computer graphics.
It depicts how the rays of consciousness of the Universe grows from the psyhodelic acrylic painting "Eden" created in 2012 by Agni Dasein.
The artist works not only in fine arts media (such as painting and performance) as well as new medial art (such as computer graphics, computer animation, VR and AR art).
