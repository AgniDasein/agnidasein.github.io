SOCIAL MEDIA ART

The project conveys social sentiment through a fictional character Rilakkuma bear, produced by the Japanese company. The reporter's photo shoot took place in 24 Stations of the Saint Petersburg Metro.
It allows to observe the different states of society related to the history and culture of the country, as well as the impact of current policies.
