Actionism

The artist has created a painting, as a symbol of human life.
She has wrapped the work in a black veil and a metal wire covered the way of kind through the four institutions of society:
education, hospital, state, religion.
After these actions, the work was presented as a symbolic gift to artist's parents. With this project, artist thanked her parents for the freedom of choice to be herself to resist the complexity of world.
